Householder Insurance — Policy Summary
Leadway Assurance Company Limited

The Leadway Householder Policy provides comprehensive protection for your home and contents. Below is a summary of the five sections of cover.

Section I — Loss or Damage to Buildings
Covers the structure of your home including walls, roof, floors, fixtures, and fittings against insured perils. Sum insured equals the full rebuild cost of the property.
Section II — Loss or Damage to Contents
Covers household goods, furniture, electronics, clothing, and personal effects within the premises. Single article limit is 3% of contents sum insured unless separately declared.
Section III — Alternative Accommodation & Loss of Rent
If your home becomes uninhabitable following an insured loss, we cover reasonable hotel and alternative accommodation costs up to 10% of Building SI + 10% of Content SI.
Section IV — Liability to the Public
Provides legal liability cover for bodily injury or property damage caused to third parties arising from the insured premises. Limit: ₦250,000 per event.
Section V — Compensation for Death of Insured
In the event of the insured's death resulting from an insured peril, a lump sum of ₦25,000 or half the total sum insured (whichever is greater) is paid to the estate.

Key Conditions

  • The insured must maintain the property in good repair throughout the policy period.
  • All claims must be reported within 30 days of the loss occurrence.
  • The insured shall take all reasonable precautions to prevent loss or damage.
  • Average clause applies — underinsurance will result in proportional settlement.
  • Excess: First ₦10,000 or 10% of claim (whichever is greater) per occurrence.
